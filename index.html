Menu

Appetizers

  • French Onion Soup :

    Rich beef broth filled with caramelized onions, topped with a baguette slice and melted Gruyère cheese.

  • Crispy Calamari :

    Lightly breaded and fried squid rings served with a side of spicy marinara sauce and a lemon wedge.

  • Classic Bruschetta :

    Toasted baguette slices topped with a fresh mixture of diced tomatoes, garlic, basil, and extra virgin olive oil.

  • Caprese Salad :

    Layers of fresh mozzarella cheese and vine-ripened tomatoes, garnished with fresh basil leaves and a balsamic glaze.

Main Courses

  • Pepperoni Pizza :

    A crispy crust topped with zesty tomato sauce, generous layers of mozzarella cheese, and spicy pepperoni slices.

  • Spaghetti Carbonara :

    Classic Italian pasta dish made with eggs, Pecorino Romano cheese, pancetta, and freshly ground black pepper.

  • Grilled Salmon :

    Fresh salmon fillet grilled to perfection, served with a lemon butter sauce, roasted vegetables, and garlic mashed potatoes.

  • Crispy Chicken Sandwich :

    A buttermilk-fried chicken breast served with pickles and creamy mayonnaise on a soft potato bun.

Desserts

  • Tiramisu :

    Classic Italian dessert made with layers of coffee-soaked ladyfingers, mascarpone cheese, and a dusting of cocoa powder.

  • Chocolate Lava Cake :

    Warm chocolate cake with a gooey molten center, served with a scoop of vanilla ice cream.

  • New York Cheesecake :

    Creamy and rich cheesecake with a graham cracker crust, topped with fresh strawberries or a drizzle of chocolate sauce.

  • Crème Brûlée :

    Silky smooth custard topped with a layer of caramelized sugar, served with fresh berries.
